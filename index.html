Fortune Cookie App

Daily fortune cookie wisdom delivered automatically to TikTok

About the App

Fortune Cookie App generates short, inspirational fortune cookie videos and uploads them automatically as drafts to your TikTok creator account using the TikTok Content Posting API. Authenticate once with TikTok Login Kit and let the app do the rest.

Features

🥠

Auto-generates fortune cookie phrases

🎬

Creates short videos automatically

📤

Uploads drafts to TikTok via API

🔒

Secure OAuth 2.0 login with TikTok

How It Works

1. Sign in with your TikTok creator account.
2. The app generates a fortune cookie video.
3. The video is uploaded as a draft to your TikTok inbox.
4. Review and publish the draft whenever you are ready.
