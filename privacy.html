Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

This app accesses your TikTok account solely to publish videos on your behalf. We do not collect or store personal data beyond what is necessary to authenticate with TikTok.

2. How We Use Information

TikTok OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device and used only to upload videos. We do not share your data with third parties.

3. Data Retention

Access tokens are stored locally and can be deleted at any time by removing the tiktok_config.json file.

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the TikTok API. Please review TikTok's own privacy policy at tiktok.com.
